Andrew Preston

Lives here. As of February 2012 he can be found in Wellington, New Zealand. This particular Andrew holds a doctorate in condensed matter physics and has three passports. He claims to be a fan of good food and cooking with friends and family, red wine, a Central Otago pinot noir perhaps, beautiful prose as exemplified by The Quiet Girl by Peter Hoeg, Tender is the Night by F. Scott Fitzgerald, and East of Eden by John Steinbeck, competitive sport and masters like the New England Patriots, the Detroit Red Wings, and Roger Federer, new ideas and thus everything Robin Hanson writes, light planes (about fifteen hours logged), and travel. He intends to see the Northern Lights from a dog sled in Greenland, watch the moon rise from the top of Kilimanjaro, and laze on the French Riviera. What a cliché.
